Hannah Tran

Theater, Film, and Digital Production Major

In 2022, I graduated from Redlands High School. Currently, I am a first year Theater, Film, and Digital Production major at the University of California, Riverside and recently, I have even been admitted into the school's Honors Program. With this major, I am studying acting so that one day I can become a successful and professional actress. In addition to this, I am also interested in psychology so I might pursue a Bachelor’s Degree in Psychology in the future.

At Redlands High School, I was involved in theater for 3 years. I acted in various productions such as “A Bond From Beyond”, “The Brothers Grimm Spectaculathon”, “Oz”, and “Grease”. In addition to this, I also wrote, directed, casted, and produced my own play called “Clueless” that was shown at the Children’s Theater Festival at Redlands High School. During my senior year, I was also elected as the Promotion and Media Director for my school’s Theater Arts Company. Besides theater, I was also involved in other extracurricular activities such as tutoring elementary kids and being in my school’s Link Crew program. Despite my multiple activities, I also focused on my studies and was in numerous honors and AP classes, which allowed me to receive various scholarships and to graduate with High Honors.

Besides my academic career, I also have had experience in a few part-time jobs. In the past, I have worked as a barista and cashier at the company Juice it Up! in Redlands CA. Currently, I am a barista at Ding Tea in Loma Linda CA.
